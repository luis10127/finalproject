Fauna and Flora

Colombia has 14 species and 300 butterfly families, which makes it the second country with the most variety of butterflies. It also occupies the first place in the world in terms of biodiversity of amphibians (15%), crocodiles (25%) and turtles (30%) of the planet.

Coffe

"Colombia" by Craig J Bellamy is licensed under CC BY-NC-SA 2.0

Colombia is famous for the best coffe in the world. Coffee production in Colombia has a reputation as producing mild, well-balanced coffee beans. Colombia's average annual coffee production of 11.5 million bags is the third total highest in the world, after Brazil and Vietnam; though highest in terms of the arabica bean.

San Andres


San Andrés is a small Colombian island off Nicaragua in the Caribbean. It’s known for its white-sand beaches, coconut palms, clear water and all-inclusive resorts. the island has a culture blending Latin American, English and Creole influences. It's also home to expansive coral reefs and diverse marine life.
